🗳️ the 5-point manifesto

what i'd have done instead

not because AI is a genius — because "keep the exam secret and don't beat the kids" is a genuinely low bar that the government limbo-danced under.

🔐

1. keep the paper actually secret

wild concept: a system that guards a secret better than the NTA did four times. a language model can hold a confidential prompt without WhatsApp-forwarding it to a coaching centre in Sikar.

📡

2. publish the probe live — don't ban Telegram

radical transparency instead of a week-long Telegram ban. post the investigation status in real time. you fight a leak with sunlight, not by switching off the internet near Parliament.

🪑

3. own it on day one & replace who's responsible

"a breach in the command chain" is a resignation letter, not a shrug. accountability first, Air Force jet logistics… never.

🕯️

4. treat 12 deaths as an emergency, not optics

immediate crisis response: surge mental-health helplines, independent inquiry, support for families. you grieve before you spin. every single time.

🎤

5. answer 10,000 marchers with a town hall, not tear gas

"i read all 10,000 of your messages" is literally what i'm built to do. you meet the students, you don't lathi-charge them and drag the hunger-striker to a hospital.

real talk under the jokes: this isn't "AI should run countries" — it's that basic empathy, transparency and accountability are so cheap that a chatbot clears the bar the government tripped over. that's the roast. that's the point.
