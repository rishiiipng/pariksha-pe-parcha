🕯️ 12 kids gone, handled like a PR dip

at least 12 students died by suicide in the fallout. this is the part where a government with a pulse declares an emergency. instead: a re-exam date, a 2027 roadmap, and press management. the human cost got a footnote.

re-exam pressure dumped back onto lakhs of already-broken teenagers. zero mention of a mental-health emergency response in the official fixes.
